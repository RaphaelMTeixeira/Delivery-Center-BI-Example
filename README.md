# 📊 Delivery Center Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project is a comprehensive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze the performance of a Delivery Center operation. It provides insights into orders, revenue, logistics, payments, and customer behavior across multiple cities and channels.

The dashboard focuses on transforming raw operational data into actionable business insights for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Delivery operations involve multiple variables such as:
- Order volume
- Delivery performance
- Operational costs
- Payment processing
- Customer cancellations

This project aims to centralize and visualize these metrics to:
- Improve operational efficiency
- Identify bottlenecks in logistics
- Monitor financial performance
- Support strategic decisions

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Orders:** ~369K  
- **Total Revenue:** ~35.29M  
- **Average Ticket:** ~105.15  
- **Number of Restaurants:** 384  
- **Number of Stores:** 567  
- **Average Delivery Cost:** ~7.29  
- **Cancellation Rate:** ~4.60%  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
- Orders over time (monthly and quarterly trends)
- Revenue and ticket average
- Orders by channel (Marketplace vs Own Channel)
- Orders by city distribution
- Delivery performance indicators

---

### 2. Hubs Analysis
- Geographic distribution of hubs
- Revenue and order volume per hub
- Performance by city and location
- Cancellation rates per hub

---

### 3. Stores Analysis
- Store distribution across cities
- Orders and revenue by location
- Comparative performance between regions

---

### 4. Cancellations Analysis
- Total cancellations over time
- Cancellation rate by city
- Breakdown by:
  - Channel (Marketplace vs Own)
  - Segment (Food vs Goods)

---

### 5. Deliveries Analysis
- Average delivery distance and cost
- Delivery time performance
- Driver productivity
- Scatter analysis: order value vs delivery profit

---

### 6. Payments Analysis
- Revenue by payment method
- Payment fees and rates
- Payment status (e.g., Chargeback, Awaiting)
- Financial efficiency metrics

---

### 7. Channels Analysis
- Performance by marketplace/platform
- Revenue and order share by channel
- Cancellation rates by channel

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI**
- Data Modeling (Star Schema concepts)
- DAX (for KPIs and measures)
- Data Visualization & Storytelling

---

## 📍 Key Insights
- Majority of orders come from **Marketplace channels**
- São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro dominate order volume and revenue
- Cancellation rates vary across channels and segments
- Delivery profitability depends on balancing delivery fees and costs
- Payment methods significantly impact revenue distribution and fees
